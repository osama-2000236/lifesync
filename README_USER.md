# LifeSync — User Guide

Welcome to **LifeSync**, your AI-powered smart life management system. This guide walks you through getting started, logging your first entries, and understanding your dashboard.

---

## 1. Creating Your Account

LifeSync uses a secure two-step verification process:

1. **Enter your email** → Click "Send Code"
2. **Check your inbox** → Enter the 6-digit verification code
3. **Choose username & password** → Your account is ready!

> Password requirements: at least 8 characters, one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

---

## 2. Your First Conversation

The **LifeSync Assistant** is the heart of the app. Instead of filling out forms, you simply tell it about your day in natural language.

### Logging Health Data

Try any of these messages:

| What you type | What gets logged |
|---|---|
| "I walked 8000 steps today" | Steps: 8,000 |
| "Slept 7 hours last night" | Sleep: 7 hours |
| "Feeling great, mood 8 out of 10" | Mood: 8/10 |
| "Drank 6 glasses of water" | Water: ~1.5L |
| "Ran for 30 minutes" | Exercise: 30 min |

### Logging Financial Data

| What you type | What gets logged |
|---|---|
| "Spent $15 on lunch" | Expense: $15.00, Food & Dining |
| "Got paid $3000 salary" | Income: $3,000.00 |
| "Paid $45 for gas" | Expense: $45.00, Transportation |
| "Bought groceries for $80" | Expense: $80.00, Groceries |

### Multi-Entry Messages

You can log multiple things at once:

> "Slept 7 hours, walked 10000 steps, and spent $12 on coffee"

This creates three separate entries automatically.

### Cross-Domain Tracking

When health and finance overlap, LifeSync links them:

> "Spent $50 on a healthy dinner"

This logs both a $50 expense AND a nutrition entry, linked together for insight analysis.

---

## 3. Clarification Prompts

If your message is ambiguous, the Assistant asks for clarification with **quick-action buttons**:

**Example:**
- You: "I spent 10"
- Assistant: "What was the $10 for?" → **[Food]** **[Transport]** **[Shopping]** **[Other]**
- Tap a button to complete the entry instantly!

Other clarification scenarios:
- "50 for gym" → "Log as: **Expense**, **Exercise**, or **Both**?"
- "I went running" → "How long or how far?"
- "Feeling okay" → Automatically logs mood 5/10 (with option to adjust)

---

## 4. Reading Your Dashboard

The **Unified Dashboard** shows your health and financial overview at a glance:

### Quick Stats (Top Row)
Four cards showing: Steps Today, Average Sleep, Mood Rating, Water Intake

### Health Trends Chart
A multi-line chart showing your **Steps** (green) and **Sleep** (purple) over the past 7 days. Look for patterns — do you walk more on days you sleep well?

### Spending Breakdown
Toggle between:
- **Doughnut view**: See your expense categories as proportional slices
- **Bar view**: Compare categories side by side

The income/expense pills show your weekly totals at a glance.

### Mood vs. Activity Scatter
Each dot represents one day. The X-axis shows activity minutes, Y-axis shows mood rating. Color indicates mood level (green = great, yellow = okay, red = low). Look for upward trends — activity often boosts mood!

### AI Insight Cards (Right Panel)
These are generated by the **Insight Engine** which analyzes your data for hidden patterns:

- **Weekly Summary**: Your AI-written health + finance overview
- **Health/Finance Scores**: 0-100 composite scores
- **Cross-Domain Insights**: Correlations between health behaviors and spending
- **Recommendations**: Actionable suggestions based on your data

---

## 5. Browsing Your History

### Health Logs Page
- Filter by type: Steps, Sleep, Mood, Water, Exercise, Nutrition, Heart Rate
- Search through notes
- Delete entries you no longer need

### Finance Logs Page
- Filter: All, Expenses only, Income only
- Search descriptions
- Each entry shows category, amount, and date

## 6. Connecting Fitness Trackers

LifeSync can sync with **Google Fit** and **Apple Health**:

1. Go to Dashboard → Settings (coming soon)
2. Click "Connect Google Fit" → Authorize via your Google account
3. Your Steps, Calories, and Sleep data will auto-sync daily

---

## 7. Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific**: "Spent $15 on lunch at a cafe" gives better categorization than "spent 15"
- **Log daily**: The AI Insight Engine needs at least 3-4 days of data to detect meaningful patterns
- **Use natural language**: Write as if texting a friend. The NLP handles the rest!
- **Check insights weekly**: The cross-domain analysis gets smarter with more data
